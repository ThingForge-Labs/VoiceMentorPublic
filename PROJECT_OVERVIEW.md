## What is VoiceMentor?

VoiceMentor is an **AI-powered voice coaching platform** that helps users improve their spoken English communication skills through structured practice, automated analysis, and personalized coaching. Think of it as a personal speech coach available 24/7 on any device.

### The Problem It Solves

Traditional spoken English training faces several challenges:

- **Subjective Assessment:** Trainers evaluate based on impression, not measurable data
- **Inconsistent Feedback:** Different trainers provide different guidance
- **Limited Practice Time:** Students get minimal speaking practice in class
- **No Progress Visibility:** Parents and corporate clients can't see tangible improvement
- **Scaling Challenges:** Institutes can't maintain quality as they grow

### The VoiceMentor Solution

VoiceMentor transforms spoken English training into a **data-driven, measurable experience**:

- **Objective Scoring:** Every session scored across six dimensions on a 0-100 scale
- **Instant Feedback:** AI analysis provides results within seconds
- **Unlimited Practice:** Students can practice daily without trainer availability constraints
- **Progress Tracking:** Visual dashboards show improvement over time
- **Trainer Amplification:** Institutes can scale while maintaining quality

### Tagline

> *"You show data. Others show opinions."*

### Step-by-Step Journey

#### Step 1: Onboarding (First-Time Users)

New users complete a **3-step personalization process**:

1. **Goal Selection ("INITIALIZATION")**
   - Choose from 8 training modules (or select multiple)
   - Options include: Reduce Filler Words, Improve Pronunciation, Increase Fluency, Enhance Vocabulary, Better Intonation, Grammar Correction, Pace Control, Confidence Boost
   - Goals personalize the coaching experience and weight dimensions accordingly

2. **Topic Selection ("CONFIGURATION")**
   - Choose a practice topic for the baseline recording
   - Preset topics: Introduce Yourself, Explain a Complex Topic, Storytelling Exercise, Debate an Argument, Sales Pitch, Random Prompt
   - Or enter a custom topic

3. **Baseline Recording ("CALIBRATION")**
   - Record 1-2 minutes speaking about the chosen topic
   - Establishes personal benchmark across all 6 dimensions
   - AI analyzes the recording to create starting metrics
   - Unlocks the main application after completion

#### Step 2: Regular Practice Sessions

After onboarding, users follow the daily practice loop:

1. **Select Topic** — Choose from presets or enter custom topic
2. **Record** — Speak for 30 seconds to 5 minutes
3. **Analyze** — AI processes the recording automatically
4. **Review** — Examine scores, transcript, and feedback
5. **Improve** — Follow personalized coaching recommendations

## The Six Dimensions of Speech Assessment

Every VoiceMentor recording is analyzed across **six dimensions of spoken English proficiency**. Each dimension receives a score from 0-100.

### Dimension Overview

| Dimension | What It Measures | Why It Matters |
|-----------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Accuracy** | How correctly words are pronounced | Clear pronunciation ensures your message is understood |
| **Fluency** | How smoothly you speak — pacing, pauses, flow | Smooth speech builds listener confidence and engagement |
| **Prosody** | Intonation, stress patterns, and natural rhythm | Good prosody makes speech sound natural and expressive |
| **Vocabulary** | Word choice, variety, and use of advanced words | Rich vocabulary allows precise, impactful communication |
| **Grammar** | Grammatical correctness and sentence structure variety | Proper grammar builds credibility and clarity |
| **Coherence** | Logical flow, structure, and idea development | Coherent speech ensures your message is organized and persuasive |

### Detailed Dimension Breakdown

#### Accuracy (Pronunciation Precision)

**What it measures:**
- Correctness of word pronunciation
- Phoneme-level accuracy
- Clarity of speech sounds

**What a high score indicates:**
- Words are pronounced correctly and understandably
- Minimal phonetic errors that could confuse listeners
- Speech is clear even to unfamiliar listeners

**What a low score indicates:**
- Frequent mispronunciations
- Sounds that may confuse listeners
- Need for focused pronunciation practice

#### Fluency (Speech Flow)

**What it measures:**
- Speech rate and pacing
- Presence and duration of pauses
- Smoothness of delivery

**What a high score indicates:**
- Natural, comfortable speaking pace
- Appropriate pausing for breath and emphasis
- Minimal filler sounds (um, uh, er)

**What a low score indicates:**
- Excessive hesitations and false starts
- Unnatural pacing (too fast or too slow)
- Frequent filler sounds disrupting flow

#### Prosody (Intonation and Rhythm)

**What it measures:**
- Pitch variation and intonation patterns
- Stress placement on key words
- Natural speech melody and rhythm

**What a high score indicates:**
- Varied, engaging intonation
- Proper stress on important words
- Natural-sounding speech melody

**What a low score indicates:**
- Monotone or flat delivery
- Missing or inappropriate stress
- Robotic or disengaged speech

#### Vocabulary (Word Choice and Variety)

**What it measures:**
- Diversity of vocabulary used
- Appropriateness of word choices
- Use of advanced or precise vocabulary

**What a high score indicates:**
- Rich, varied word choice
- Avoidance of repetition
- Natural use of advanced vocabulary

**What a low score indicates:**
- Limited vocabulary range
- Over-reliance on basic words
- Frequent repetition of the same terms

#### Grammar (Grammatical Accuracy)

**What it measures:**
- Correctness of grammatical structures
- Variety of sentence patterns
- Proper use of tenses, articles, and agreements

**What a high score indicates:**
- Grammatically correct speech
- Varied sentence structures
- Proper use of complex grammatical forms

**What a low score indicates:**
- Frequent grammatical errors
- Limited sentence variety
- Basic or incorrect grammatical structures

#### Coherence (Logical Organization)

**What it measures:**
- Clear structure and organization
- Logical flow of ideas
- Effective use of transitions and connectors

**What a high score indicates:**
- Well-organized thoughts
- Clear beginning, middle, and end
- Smooth transitions between ideas

**What a low score indicates:**
- Disorganized or rambling speech
- Missing connections between ideas
- Weak or absent conclusions

## Standard Mode vs Advanced Mode

VoiceMentor offers two distinct analysis pipelines designed for different use cases and cost structures. Both modes provide full six-dimension feedback.

### Standard Mode

**Also known as:** Content-focused analysis, Single-model analysis

**How it works:**
- Uses a single AI model optimized for content analysis
- Processes speech transcript and linguistic patterns
- Provides full six-dimension assessment
- Lower cost per recording

**Best for:**
- Daily high-frequency practice
- Cost-conscious learners
- B2C subscription tiers (Starter, Essential)
- Users focused on vocabulary, grammar, and coherence improvement

**What it analyzes:**
| Dimension | Analysis Source | Strength |
|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| Accuracy | Estimated from transcript patterns | Identifies common pronunciation errors |
| Fluency | Speech rate and pause patterns from text | Detects filler words and hesitations |
| Prosody | Inferred from text structure | Identifies basic intonation issues |
| Vocabulary | Full lexical analysis | Advanced vocabulary detection |
| Grammar | Comprehensive grammar check | Grammar error identification |
| Coherence | Logical flow assessment | Structure and organization scoring |

**Cost:** Approximately 0.14 INR per recording (~26x cheaper than Advanced Mode)

---

### Advanced Mode

**Also known as:** Bi-modal analysis, Dual-model analysis, Enterprise-grade assessment

**How it works:**
- Uses two complementary AI systems
- Combines specialized speech analysis with content understanding
- Provides enhanced pronunciation assessment
- Higher cost per recording but more precise feedback

**Best for:**
- Enterprise and institute plans
- Users prioritizing pronunciation accuracy
- Pro and Elite B2C tiers
- Assessment and certification scenarios

**What it analyzes:**
| Dimension | Analysis Source | Strength |
|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| Accuracy | Specialized speech recognition engine | Phoneme-level pronunciation accuracy |
| Fluency | Specialized speech recognition engine | Precise timing, pause detection, speech rate |
| Prosody | Specialized speech recognition engine | Pitch analysis, stress detection, intonation |
| Vocabulary | Content analysis engine | Advanced vocabulary detection |
| Grammar | Content analysis engine | Grammar error identification |
| Coherence | Content analysis engine | Structure and organization scoring |

**What makes it "Advanced":**

1. **Phoneme-Level Precision:** Detects exact sound mispronunciations, not just word-level errors
2. **Prosodic Analysis:** Measures pitch, stress, and intonation directly from audio
3. **Temporal Accuracy:** Precise timing measurements for fluency assessment
4. **Enterprise Standards:** Meets the accuracy requirements of professional training institutes

**Cost:** Approximately 3.73 INR per recording

---

### Mode Comparison

| Aspect | Standard Mode | Advanced Mode |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| **Analysis Pipeline** | Single AI model | Two complementary systems |
| **Pronunciation Assessment** | Word-level accuracy | Phoneme-level precision |
| **Prosody Assessment** | Inferred from text | Direct audio analysis |
| **Cost Per Recording** | ~0.14 INR | ~3.73 INR |
| **Best Use Case** | Daily practice, high volume | Detailed assessment, certification |
| **Typical User** | Individual learners, B2C tiers | Institutes, Pro/Elite users |
| **Dimensions Covered** | All 6 dimensions | All 6 dimensions (enhanced pronunciation) |
| **Feedback Quality** | Excellent for content improvement | Superior for pronunciation work |

### When to Use Each Mode

**Choose Standard Mode when:**
- You're building a daily practice habit
- You want to practice frequently without high costs
- Your primary focus is vocabulary, grammar, and coherence
- You're on the Starter or Essential tier

**Choose Advanced Mode when:**
- You're preparing for an important presentation or interview
- Pronunciation is your priority improvement area
- You need the most detailed assessment possible
- You're on the Pro or Elite tier, or enrolled through an institute

## What VoiceMentor Measures and Tracks

VoiceMentor provides comprehensive measurement and tracking across multiple dimensions of the learning journey.

### Per-Session Metrics

Every recording generates the following immediate metrics:

| Metric Category | Specific Metrics |
|-----------------|------------------|
| **Dimension Scores** | Accuracy (0-100), Fluency (0-100), Prosody (0-100), Vocabulary (0-100), Grammar (0-100), Coherence (0-100) |
| **Overall Score** | Weighted composite score (0-100) based on user's goal priorities |
| **Letter Grade** | A+ (95-100), A (90-94), B+ (85-89), B (80-84), C+ (75-79), C (70-74), D (60-69), F (0-59) |
| **Duration** | Recording length in seconds (minimum 30s, maximum 5 minutes) |
| **Word Count** | Total words spoken |
| **Speech Rate** | Words per minute |
| **Habits Detected** | List of speech patterns identified in this session |
| **Transcript** | Full text of speech with color-coded highlights (errors, fillers, good usage) |

### Progress Tracking Over Time

| Metric Type | What's Tracked | Time Window |
|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| **Dimension Trends** | Score history for each of 6 dimensions | All time, with sparkline for last 10 sessions |
| **Overall Score Trend** | Composite score progression | All time |
| **Total Recordings** | Cumulative count of all sessions | All time |
| **Total Practice Minutes** | Sum of all recording durations | All time |
| **Practice Streak** | Consecutive days of practice | Current and best streak |
| **Last Practice Date** | Most recent recording date | For engagement tracking |

### Speech Habits Tracking

VoiceMentor detects **40+ distinct speech habits** across **7 categories**:

| Category | Example Habits | How Persistence Is Tracked |
|----------|----------------|---------------------------|
| **Filler/Crutch Words** | "um," "uh," "like," "you know" | Counted per session, flagged at 3+ uses |
| **Fluency/Flow** | Long pauses, false starts, repetitions | Detected when patterns exceed thresholds |
| **Prosody/Delivery** | Monotone, rushed pacing, inconsistent volume | Identified through audio analysis |
| **Structure/Coherence** | Tangential responses, missing conclusions | Assessed via content organization |
| **Vocabulary/Word Choice** | Word repetition, informal register | Tracked via lexical analysis |
| **Grammar Patterns** | Subject-verb disagreement, tense inconsistency | Counted per session |
| **Pragmatic/Discourse** | Inappropriate hedging, over-qualification | Identified via discourse analysis |

**Persistent Habits:** A habit is marked as "persistent" when it appears in **3 or more sessions**, triggering targeted coaching cards.

### Goal-Specific Metrics

Users can track their progress against **custom goals**:

| Goal Type | What's Measured | How It's Displayed |
|-----------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Predefined Goals** | Score calculated with goal-specific weights | Collapsible "Goal breakdown" on dashboard showing per-goal scores |
| **Custom Goals** | AI-generated personalized metrics | Individual goal cards with scores and top dimensions |
| **Main Goal Priority** | Primary goal gets 2x weight in scoring | Highlighted as "Main Goal" in goal breakdown |

### Level Progression Tracking

VoiceMentor uses a **5-level proficiency system**:

| Level | Name | Minimum Score | Minimum Sessions | Progress Display |
|-------|------|---------------|------------------|------------------|
| 1 | Beginning | 0 | 0 | Gray indicator |
| 2 | Improving | 40+ | 5+ | Blue indicator with progress bar |
| 3 | Proficient | 55+ | 10+ | Green indicator with progress bar |
| 4 | Advanced | 70+ | 15+ | Amber indicator with progress bar |
| 5 | Expert | 85+ | 20+ | Purple indicator with progress bar |

**Progress Requirements:**
- BOTH score threshold AND session count must be met
- Progress bar shows how close user is to next level
- Level is designed to reward consistency, not one-time performance

### Institute-Level Tracking (B2B)

For institutes, additional aggregate metrics are available:

| Metric | Description | Granularity |

| **Accent Reduction** | **30%** | 15% | **25%** | 10% | 10% | 10% |
| **Business English** | 10% | 15% | 10% | **20%** | **25%** | **20%** |

**Reading the weights:** For Job Interview, Grammar (25%) and Coherence (20%) are weighted highest because interviewers evaluate structured, grammatically correct responses. For Accent Reduction, Accuracy (30%) and Prosody (25%) dominate because pronunciation and intonation are the primary focus.

### Weight Blending for Multiple Goals

When users select multiple goals:

1. **Main Goal Priority:** The user-designated "main goal" gets **2x influence** in the weight calculation
2. **Secondary Goals:** Other selected goals contribute equally
3. **Normalization:** All weights are normalized to sum to 1.0

**Example:**
- User selects: "Ace job interviews" (main), "Improve pronunciation", "Increase fluency"
- "Ace job interviews" weights are multiplied by 2
- Other two goals' weights are multiplied by 1
- Result is normalized to produce final dimension weights

### Custom Goal Creation

Users can enter **custom goals** in their own words. The AI:

1. Analyzes the natural language description
2. Generates appropriate dimension weights
3. Creates a personalized weight table for that goal
4. Applies it alongside predefined goals in the calculation

**Example custom goals:**
- "I want to stop saying 'basically' and 'literally' every sentence"
- "I need to sound more confident in team meetings"
- "I want to stop rushing when I'm nervous"

### Goal Updates and Evolution

Users can **update their goals at any time** through Settings:

- Add new goals as priorities shift
- Remove completed goals
- Change the main goal designation
- Goals affect scoring going forward (historical scores are preserved with original weights)

## Speech Habits Detection

VoiceMentor's habit detection system identifies **40+ distinct speech patterns** across **7 categories**. This system builds self-awareness, which is the first step toward improvement.

### Habit Categories and Examples

| Category | Description | Example Habits | Detection Method |
|----------|-------------|----------------|------------------|
| **Filler/Crutch Words** | Unnecessary vocalized pauses and filler expressions | "um," "uh," "like," "you know," "basically" | Transcript word counting |
| **Fluency/Flow** | Smoothness and continuity of speech | Long pauses, false starts, repetitions, self-corrections | Audio timing + transcript |
| **Prosody/Delivery** | Intonation, stress, and vocal variety | Monotone pitch, rushed pacing, inconsistent volume | Audio analysis |
| **Structure/Coherence** | Organization and logical flow | Tangential responses, missing conclusions, weak transitions | Content analysis |
| **Vocabulary/Word Choice** | Word selection and variety | Word repetition, informal register, limited range | Lexical analysis |
| **Grammar Patterns** | Grammatical correctness patterns | Subject-verb disagreement, tense inconsistency | Grammar checking |
| **Pragmatic/Discourse** | Conversational patterns and discourse markers | Inappropriate hedging, over-qualification | Discourse analysis |

### Persistence Tracking

A habit is marked as **"persistent"** when:

1. It appears in **3 or more consecutive sessions**
2. The pattern is consistent across recordings
3. It meets the threshold criteria for that habit type

**What Happens When Habits Persist:**

- Habit badges appear on the dashboard (e.g., `[FAST_PACER ×8]`)
- **Facilitator coaching cards** are triggered with targeted exercises
- Priority tasks include habit-specific recommendations
- Trends section shows habit frequency over time

### High-Priority Habits (Most Common & Actionable)

These habits are prioritized for detection because they are:
- **Highly detectable** by AI
- **Very common** across speakers
- **Highly actionable** (improvable with practice)

| Habit | Description | Why It Matters |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| **FILLER_SOUND** | "um," "uh," "ah" usage | Universally common, easily replaced with silence |
| **FILLER_WORD** | "like," "basically," "literally" | Makes speakers sound less confident |
| **FILLER_PHRASE** | "you know," "I mean," "to be honest" | Signature filler phrases are easy to eliminate |
| **HEDGING_LANGUAGE** | "kind of," "sort of," "maybe," "perhaps" | Reduces speaker authority and impact |
| **WEAK_OPENING** | Throat-clearing, apologies, excessive preamble | First impressions matter in speaking |
| **WEAK_CLOSING** | Trailing off, "so yeah," incomplete endings | Undermines message impact |
| **RAMBLING** | Extended speech without clear point | Reduces clarity and audience engagement |

## Progression and Levels

VoiceMentor's level system rewards **consistent practice**, not just raw talent.

### The 5-Level System

| Level | Name | Minimum Score | Minimum Sessions | Color |
|-------|------|---------------|------------------|-------|
| 1 | **Beginning** | 0 | 0 | Gray |
| 2 | **Improving** | 40+ | 5+ | Blue |
| 3 | **Proficient** | 55+ | 10+ | Green |
| 4 | **Advanced** | 70+ | 15+ | Amber |
| 5 | **Expert** | 85+ | 20+ | Purple |

### Dual Requirements

**To level up, users must meet BOTH criteria:**

1. **Score Threshold:** Achieve the minimum average score
2. **Session Count:** Complete the minimum number of practice sessions

**Why both?**

- **Score alone** could be achieved with one lucky recording
- **Sessions alone** doesn't ensure skill improvement
- **Together** they verify sustained improvement, not a one-time peak

### Progress Display

The dashboard shows:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ LEVEL: IMPROVING              ▓▓▓▓░░ 62%    │
│                                              │
│ Next: Proficient (55 score, 10 sessions)     │
│                                              │
│ Current: Average 48, 7 sessions             │
│ Need: +7 points OR 3 more sessions           │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Scoring Algorithm: Weighted Moving Average

Your level is calculated using a **weighted moving average** that balances:

- **40% weight** on your last 3 sessions (recent performance)
- **30% weight** on your last 5 sessions (sustained quality)
- **30% weight** on your all-time best score (peak achievement)

**This approach:**
- Rewards consistent recent performance
- Doesn't penalize one off day
- Recognizes your best achievements
